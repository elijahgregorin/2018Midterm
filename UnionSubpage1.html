Important Union Victories

Savannah

At the battle in Savannah, the Union won versus the Confederates. William Sherman, general of a Union Army, presented the win of Savannah to Abraham as a Christmas present.

Battle of Shiloh

The battle of Shiloh took place in Tennessee when the Confederates surprised the Union Troops. The Union almost lost, but then reinforcements arrived and they won. It was a very costly victory.

Battle at Gettysburg

This battle took place in south central Pennslavania. It is one of the most famous Union victories because Lincoln gave the two minute Gettysburg address here.

Vicksburg

Though this wasn't a actual battle, it is an important Union victory. General Grant laid siege on the fort at Vicksburg and its capture gave the Union complete control of the Mississippi river.

Antietam(Sharpsburg)

This was a Union victory that resulted in the greatest one-day toll of casulties in the entire Civil war.

Lookout Mountain

This was won by the Union who climbed up sheer cliffs to fight the Confederates
