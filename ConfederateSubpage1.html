Important Confederate Victories

Fort Sumpter

This was captured shortly after Abraham Lincoln was elected. The Confederates laid siege on the fort and then they attacked the fort. The general has to surrender, because they had no food.

Chickamauga

This is the battle that took place just before Lookout Mountain. In this battle, the Union troops attacked in Northern Georgia. When they attacked, they beat the Union so hard that they retreated to Chattanooga.

Battle of Bull Run (First and Second)

Both of these took place in Virginia. Both resulted in the Confederates winning. The Union lost many troops in both of these battles.
